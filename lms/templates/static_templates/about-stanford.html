About Stanford Online

Stanford has a range of courses accessible to the public, and you can find out more about them, and about Stanford's online initiatives, at online.stanford.edu

The engineering team at Stanford's office of the Vice Provost for Online Learning is running and developing on an instance of the edX platform, which will be open source on June 1. More details are in this press release.
